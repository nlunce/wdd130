Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wanted to be a part of a community of like-minded ambitious women like yourself? Have you ever wanted to be a part of a community of women that will help you grow and develop in the business world? Look no further! Project EmpowHer is a community made just for women who strive to become better and build better business connections. Project EmpowHer assists women in their business endeavors and provides them with an excellent networking ability to link with like-minded women.

Audience

Women from all backgrounds who are intrested in thriving in todays crazy world.

Content

Home page

What is Sisterhood? Through sisterhood we unlearn what we’ve been told about the world... That it’s a harsh place for women, where competition and jealousy rule the relationships we have with each other. To come out on the other side empowered, supported, and knowing that we’re not alone in this, and that together we can achieve anything. We are all social animals, and women especially thrive better when we have a sense of belonging, and community. Stepping into true sisterhood c means that you’re willing to go deep, and to love fully, with all that you are, visible, vulnerable. It’s a conscious choice that you have to make. Sisterhood is not something that happens to you, it’s something that you willfully create.

The Inner Circle

This Community Is For You If... You want to connect with women to achieve more clarity, feel more confident and stay accountable to your goals. You’re committed to taking action; to creating routine, structure and schedule; to holding yourself accountable by showing up, putting in the time and energy that’s needed to grow your business. You’re willing to contribute and know that investing in yourself and your business will be rewarding in the long-term. You want a support system of other women who openly talk about what has or has not worked in their business. You believe being surrounded by a network of other successful female leaders would help you stay motivated and excited to continue to grow.
