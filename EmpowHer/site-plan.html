Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wanted to be a part of a community of like-minded ambitious women like yourself? Have you ever wanted to be a part of a community of women that will help you grow and develop in the business world? Look no further! Project EmpowHer is a community made just for women who strive to become better and build better business connections. Project EmpowHer assists women in their business endeavors and provides them with an excellent networking ability to link with like-minded women.

Audience

Women from all backgrounds who are intrested in thriving in todays crazy world.
